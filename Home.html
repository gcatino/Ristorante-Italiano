Italian

Restaurant

✻

With ancestry from the traditional Catino Barontini family from the towns of Bari (Andria) and Rome, the capital of Italy, his interest and taste for gastronomy is in his blood. After passing through the Universidad Externado de Colombia and graduating as hotel and tourist company manager, Gino decides to travel to Italy to rediscover his roots and take his passion for cooking to another level.

Located in Rome, he studies gastronomy and begins his career in several classic restaurants and restaurants in the city, until arriving at the renowned kitchen of the Hotel Hassler Village, one of the most important in the country. On his return to Bogotá in 2002, he opens the doors of Italian Restaurant, with the great goal of rescuing and sharing traditional Italian cuisine.
